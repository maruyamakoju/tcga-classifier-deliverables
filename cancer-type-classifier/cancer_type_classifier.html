Error patterns are biologically plausible, not mechanistic proof. The largest off-diagonal block is READ→COAD (15 of 20). Other confusions cluster within kidney, lung/squamous, upper-GI and hepatobiliary labels, but technical or cohort effects are not excluded.

Several coefficients agree with canonical tissue markers (THCA: TG/TPO/FOXE1; PRAD: ACP3/NKX3-1/KLK4; LIHC: SHBG/AMBP/GC; LUSC: SFTPB/SFTPA1). This is qualitative consistency, not causal proof.
